User Guide

A lightweight, private tracker for your spiritual journey.

🔒 Your Privacy & Data

None of your data from this app—your notes or your timings—ever leaves your phone or computer. There is no central database and no one (including the developer) can see what you write. It stays 100% on your device, just like a paper journal.

✍️ Formatting

Use the toolbar to add bold, italics, or lists. On mobile, you can also long-press text to use your phone's native formatting menu.
